# Evaluation Basics

Evaluation is the process of measuring AI model quality, performance, and reliability before and after deployment.

Effective evaluation helps engineers understand whether AI systems meet accuracy, usability, and operational requirements.

---

# Purpose

Understand:

- Why AI models need evaluation
- Common evaluation approaches
- Relationship between metrics and real-world performance
- Importance of continuous improvement

---

# Core Concepts

## Model Performance Evaluation

Measures how well a model performs on defined tasks.

Examples:

- Prediction accuracy
- Classification performance
- Generation quality

---

## Evaluation Metrics

Metrics provide measurable indicators of model behavior.

Consider:

- Accuracy
- Precision and recall
- Response quality
- Performance characteristics

---

## Testing Approaches

AI systems can be evaluated through:

- Benchmark datasets
- Automated testing
- Human review
- Real-world feedback

---

## Production Evaluation

After deployment, evaluation continues through:

- User feedback
- Performance monitoring
- Quality tracking
- Model improvement cycles

---

# Evaluation Flow

```text
AI Model

Model Improvement
```

# AI Engineering Perspective

Reliable AI systems require:

- Clear evaluation goals
