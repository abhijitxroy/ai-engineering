# Model Lifecycle

The AI model lifecycle describes the stages involved in building, improving, deploying, and maintaining AI models.

Understanding the lifecycle helps engineers manage AI systems beyond initial development and support reliable production operation.

---

# Purpose

Understand:

- Stages of AI model development
- How models move from experimentation to production
- Importance of monitoring and improvement
- Engineering responsibilities across the lifecycle

---

# Lifecycle Stages

## Data Preparation

Purpose:

Prepare high-quality data for model development.

Activities:

- Data collection
- Data cleaning
- Data processing
- Dataset validation

---

## Model Development

Purpose:

Create and improve AI models.

Activities:

- Model selection
- Training
- Experimentation
- Parameter tuning

---

## Model Evaluation

Purpose:

Measure model quality before deployment.

Consider:

- Accuracy
- Performance
- Reliability
- Real-world suitability

---

## Deployment

Purpose:

Make AI models available for applications and users.

Considerations:

- Serving infrastructure
- Scalability
- Security
- Availability

---

## Monitoring and Improvement

Purpose:

Maintain model quality after deployment.

Activities:

- Performance monitoring
- Feedback collection
- Model updates
- Continuous improvement

---

# Model Lifecycle Flow

```text
Data Preparation

↓

Model Training

↓

Evaluation

↓

Deployment

↓

Monitoring

↓

Continuous Improvement
```
