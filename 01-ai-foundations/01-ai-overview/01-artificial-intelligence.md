# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of engineering and computer science focused on building systems that can perform tasks requiring human-like intelligence.

AI systems can analyze information, recognize patterns, make decisions, and generate outputs based on learned knowledge or predefined rules.

---

# Purpose

Understand:

- What Artificial Intelligence means
- Different categories of AI systems
- How AI differs from traditional software systems
- Where modern AI fits in engineering

---

# Core Concepts

## Traditional Software vs AI Systems

Traditional software:

- Rules are explicitly programmed
- Behavior is defined by developers
- Same input generally follows predefined logic

AI systems:

- Learn patterns from data
- Use models to make predictions or generate outputs
- Improve through training and feedback

---

## Types of AI

### Narrow AI

Systems designed for specific tasks.

Examples:

- Recommendation systems
- Speech recognition
- Image classification

---

### General AI

A theoretical concept describing AI systems with broad human-level intelligence capabilities.

# AI Engineering Perspective

Modern AI systems combine:

- Data
- Models
- Computing infrastructure
- Applications
- Operational practices

AI engineering focuses on transforming AI capabilities into reliable software systems.
