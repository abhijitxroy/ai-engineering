# Large Language Models

Large Language Models (LLMs) are AI models designed to understand and generate human language by learning patterns from large-scale text datasets.

LLMs are built using deep learning architectures, especially transformer-based models, and provide the foundation for many modern AI applications.

---

# Purpose

Understand:

- What Large Language Models are
- How LLMs process language
- How LLMs generate responses
- The role of LLMs in AI engineering

---

# Core Concepts

## Language Understanding

LLMs learn relationships between words, concepts, and patterns from large amounts of training data.

Capabilities include:

- Text understanding
- Summarization
- Question answering
- Information extraction

---

## Language Generation

LLMs generate responses by predicting and producing sequences of tokens based on learned patterns and provided context.

Examples:

- Chat assistants
- Code generation
- Content creation
- Knowledge applications

---

## Transformer Foundation

Modern LLMs are based on transformer architectures that enable:

- Efficient sequence processing
- Context understanding
- Large-scale model training
